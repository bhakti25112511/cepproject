Symptoms of Asthma

Common signs and symptoms to help you recognize asthma early.

Shortness of Breath

Difficulty breathing, especially during physical activity or at night.

Coughing

Frequent coughing, especially at night or early morning.

Wheezing

A whistling sound when exhaling, caused by narrowed airways.

Chest Tightness

Feeling of pressure or tightness in the chest, especially during attacks.
